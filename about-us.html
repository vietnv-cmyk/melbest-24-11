The Spark Behind Mulbust

At Mulbust, we believe that light is more than just illumination—it's an art form that shapes spaces and evokes emotion. Our journey began with a simple passion: to craft exquisite lighting solutions that are not merely functional but are integral pieces of art, transforming every environment they grace.

From intricate chandeliers to minimalist pendant lights, each piece is conceived with a dedication to artisanal excellence and a vision for timeless beauty.

Our Milestones

  • 2010: The Genesis

    Mulbust was founded by a team of passionate designers and engineers, united by a shared vision to bring exceptional craftsmanship to the world of lighting. Our first studio opened, focusing on bespoke, hand-forged designs.

  • 2015: Expanding Horizons

    Responding to growing demand, we inaugurated our larger artisan workshop, allowing us to expand our product line and integrate advanced, yet artisanal, manufacturing techniques. This year also marked our debut at international design fairs.

  • 2020: Sustainable Innovation

    Pioneering sustainable practices, we began incorporating recycled materials and energy-efficient LED technology into all new designs. Our commitment to eco-conscious craftsmanship was formally recognized with industry accolades.

  • Today: Illuminating Futures

    Mulbust continues to innovate, pushing the boundaries of design and sustainability. We are proud to offer a diverse collection that illuminates homes, businesses, and public spaces worldwide, always upholding our founding principles.

Our Philosophy

Craftsmanship Beyond Compare

Every Mulbust fixture is a testament to the dedication of our skilled artisans. We combine time-honored techniques with contemporary design principles, ensuring each piece is not only beautiful but also built to last for generations.

Exquisite Materials, Thoughtfully Sourced

We scour the globe for the finest materials—from ethically sourced metals and responsibly harvested woods to hand-blown glass and shimmering crystals. Our commitment to quality materials guarantees a superior finish and unparalleled durability.

A Luminous Future: Sustainability at Our Core

Mulbust is deeply committed to environmental stewardship. We prioritize sustainable manufacturing processes, minimize waste, and continually seek innovative ways to reduce our ecological footprint, creating beauty that doesn't cost the Earth.
